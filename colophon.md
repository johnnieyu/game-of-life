# COLOPHON

## METABOLIC SUBLIMES POTLUCK
THE METABOLIC SUBLIMES STUDY GROUP
IS ORGANIZED BY ADRIANA GALLO
HOSTED BY INDEX GREENPOINT IN NYC
BETWEEN JAN 17 - FEB 7, 2026

THE POTLUCK IS A FINAL PRESENTATION
OF IDEAS AND LEARNINGS FROM THE
FOUR WEEKS TOGETHER.

THE POTLUCK VERSION THE GAME OF LIFE
WAS CONCEIVED BY JOHNNIE YU TO
DEMONSTRATE MICROSCOPIC SYSTEMS
ON MULTIPLE LEVELS CONVERGING TO
CREATE COMPLEX EMERGENT BEHAVIOR
IN LARGE SYSTEMS DESPITE SIMPLE RULES.

## HISTORY
THE GAME OF LIFE WAS CREATED BY
MATHEMATICIAN JOHN CONWAY (1937-2020)
IN 1970 TO DEMONSTRATE ZERO-PLAYER
CELLULAR AUTOMATION.

## INSPIRATION
WITH PARTICULAR INSPIRATION FROM
